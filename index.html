About our organization

We are a family business, taking the world by storm in 2021. Our purpose was finding quality dessert products to satisfy our sweet cravings and from our experiments, we created and came up with Sally's Ice Cream Shop. Not only are we keeping our sweet secrets to ourselves but we are sharing it with the world. We pride ourselves in providng high-quality ingredients to provide an deliicious, yet healthy choice for our ice-cream products.

From starting at a small mom and pop shop in Tennessee, we have successfully expanded to a number of states. With our new business structure, customers are now able to order and get products shipped anywhere in the United States. We hope to continue providing consistent quality and ingredients to our customers.
